The Framework

Polya's Four-Step Method

Every problem in this bank is solved using George Pólya's systematic approach to mathematical problem solving.

🔍
Step 01
Understand
Identify what is given, what is unknown, and what the problem is asking. Restate it clearly.
🗺️
Step 02
Devise a Plan
Choose a strategy: set up equations, draw diagrams, look for patterns, or work backwards.
⚙️
Step 03
Carry Out
Execute the plan step by step, showing all calculations clearly and logically.
✅
Step 04
Review
Verify the answer satisfies all conditions and makes sense in the context of the problem.
